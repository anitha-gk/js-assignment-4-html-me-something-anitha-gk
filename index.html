    • My State

    I am from the state called Kerala in India.It is the southern most part of India. We speak a language called Malayalam in Kerala. Kerala, though a small state, is blessed with beautiful places, backwaters, beaches etc. .

    Changanacherry

    Historically, Changanacherry was a major regional centre, serving as a connecting hub. It also hosted one of the most famous markets in Kerala This is my hometown, I did all my studies and completed my Bachelor of Science Degree, all my family are living here was able to vist them during my vacation.

    Mysore

    Mysore (or Mysuru), a city in India's southwestern Karnataka state, was the former capital of the Kingdom of Mysore. In its center is opulent Mysore Palace. My husband is from Mysore and I moved to Mysore after marriage,we visited the grand Mysore Palace which is a famous attraction.

    Bangalore

    Bangalore or Bengaluru is the capital of India's southern Karnataka state. The center of India's high-tech industry, the city is also known for its parks and nightlife. My Brother is living in Bangalore and I visted him during my vacation.

    Places I visited in India during vacation
